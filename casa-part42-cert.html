The holder of this certificate is approved to provide continuing airworthiness management services subject to (i) the aircraft types and models, and (ii) the kinds of continuing airworthiness management services, and (iii) the limitations and conditions set out in the schedule below.

Legend (reference CASR Part 42):


(A) Ensuring that the requirements mentioned in Divisions 42.C.2, 42.C.3 and 42.C.4 of Part 42 of the CASR are met;
(B) Issuing an airworthiness review certificate under Division 42.I.2 of Part 42 of the CASR;
(C) Extending an airworthiness review certificate under Division 42.I.3 of Part 42 of the CASR;
(D) Carrying out of an airworthiness review under Division 42.I.4 of Part 42 of the CASR;
(E) Approving a maintenance program under Division 42.J.2 of Part 42 of the CASR;
(F) Approving a variation of an approved maintenance program under Division 42.J.4 of Part 42 of the CASR.
Conditions
The Reference Number must be used when the Civil Aviation Regulations 1988, the Civil Aviation Safety Regulations 1998, or an instrument made under any of these regulations requires a CASA reference number to be included in a Form or other document, for the purpose of the regulations or the instrument.
Locations
The holder may only conduct training and assessment at the locations listed in their Operations Manual.


Subsequent issue (renewal) of CASA.42.0000

Your CASA.42.0000 expires soon. You can apply for a no change renewal (subesequent issue) online provided there are no changes required. Note that once a certificate expires a subsequent issue cannot be given and you will have to apply for an initial issue of the CASA.42.0000
